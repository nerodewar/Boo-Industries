About Boo Industries

Home · Privacy

Founded by James Ritchie in 2025, Boo Industries is an app developer focused on delivering peace of mind for its users through mindful design ethics.

We build small, intentional experiences that reduce noise, lower friction, and help people feel calm enough to begin — whether that’s writing, reflecting, or simply taking a moment to breathe.

  • Mindful by default: minimal choices, gentle pacing, and a calm interface.
  • Privacy-respecting: we aim to collect as little as possible, and be clear about what we do.
  • Everyday usefulness: tools you can return to without effort.
